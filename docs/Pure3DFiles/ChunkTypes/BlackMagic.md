title: "Black Magic (0x1025)"
description: "Instructs The Simpsons: Road Rage to advance to the next frame of its loading screen animation."
---

A Black Magic chunk instructs The Simpsons: Road Rage to advance its loading screen animation to the next frame when it is encountered during a loading screen.

Radical placed these every 8 chunks in the game's map files and employed the above method to convey to the player that something is, in fact, happening on the loading screen.
